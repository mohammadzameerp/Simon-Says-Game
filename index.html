How to play 'Simon Says'

  • Press Start to start the game.
  • The game will show you a sequence of colors by flashing them one by one.
  • Click the colors in the same order as they were shown.
  • If you repeat the sequence correctly, you'll move to the next level. The sequence will get longer with each level.
  • At every level, you need to remember the colors from all previous levels.
  • If you click the wrong color or forget any part of the sequence, you'll lose the game.
  • Try to go as far as you can and see how long you can remember the sequences..!
